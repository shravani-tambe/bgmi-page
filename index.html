BGMI

Erangel

Erangel is the base map for the game. An 8x8 km map dotted with small outcroppings of buildings.

Miramar

Miramar is a desert map for the game with an 8x8 km map having a sheer landscape and barren land.

Sanhok

Sanhok is a 4x4km island compared to other maps that are 8x8km. In this small map, enemies will always be lurking nearby.

Vikendi

Vikendi is 6x6km map set on Adratic Sea as a fictional snow island. Watch out for the player footprints!!

Battlefield Arena Rules

1

Team Members

Each team should have 4 members at all times during the competition.

2

Designated Captain

Each team must have a designated captain who will be their point of contact with tournament officials.

3

Character Clothing

The in-game characters of all players must remain fully clothed after they leave the lobby.

4

Mobile Phones Only

Players are allowed to participate using only handheld mobile phones.

5

No Emulators

No player should use an Emulator like BlueStacks to play on a PC or any other device that is not an Android smartphone.

6

Player Changes

Teams cannot change their players during the competition.

7

Cheating Penalties

If caught or suspected cheating, the team will be eliminated unless they provide hard evidence (video recording).

8

Point System

Winners will be decided according to the point table. Total points = rank points + kill points (1 kill = 1 point).

9

POV Videos

From each team, there should be at least 2 POV videos.
